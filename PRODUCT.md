# Tony For Our Fitness

One-page marketing site for personal trainer Tony Quartini (tony4ourfitness.com).

- **Register:** brand (design IS the product; the page sells a first impression)
- **Audience:** everyday people considering 1:1 training: beginners, desk workers, post-injury returns. Not gym rats.
- **Voice:** confident, warm, premium-but-human. Equinox restraint, never bro-gym loud.
- **Brand color:** warm bronze `#B06E3B` on charcoal/off-white. Committed accent, not decoration.
- **Stack:** single static `index.html` (vanilla CSS/JS), images in `assets/`, deployed via GitHub Pages at vibenow.me/tony.
- **Constraints:** no build step, fast on phones, accessible (reduced-motion, focus states, contrast).

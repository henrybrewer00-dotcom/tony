# Tony For Our Fitness

One-page marketing site for personal trainer Tony Quartini (tony4ourfitness.com).

- **Register:** brand (design IS the product; the page sells a first impression)
- **Audience:** everyday people considering 1:1 training: beginners, desk workers, post-injury returns. Not gym rats.
- **Voice:** confident, warm, premium-but-human. Cinematic athletic, never bro-gym loud.
- **Design system ("Ember", v2):** committed dark. Warm charcoal `#131110` surface, light warm ink `#F2EDE4`, one ember accent `#D87A3A`. Archivo variable (width axis: Expanded Black display, normal body). Ember slash `/` is the recurring brand mark (wordmark + section titles). Double-bezel frames on photos/panels. Floating glass pill nav. Photo-led: real shots in assets/gallery/.
- **Motion:** masked line-rise hero, tilted photo stack entrance, scroll reveals (60ms stagger), fullscreen mobile menu with staggered links, strong quint easing, reduced-motion safe.
- **Stack:** single static `index.html` (vanilla CSS/JS), GitHub Pages at vibenow.me/tony.
- **Constraints:** no build step, fast on phones, accessible.

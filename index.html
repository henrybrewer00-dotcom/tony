About

Hi, I'm Tony Quartini.

I started training clients in 2011, and somewhere between the first squat I ever coached and today, I fell in love with one thing: watching people hit goals they didn't think were possible.

With over six years of hands-on coaching, I've worked with complete beginners, busy parents, desk-bound professionals, and athletes coming back from injury. My approach is simple — meet you where you are, build a plan that fits your life, and progress at a pace your body can sustain.

No yelling. No gimmicks. Just consistent, intelligent training — and a coach in your corner.
